# COMP110 Resource Effectiveness Analysis

## Overview

For this project, we analyzed survey data from COMP110 students to understand which course resources are most effective for learning. We focused on lesson videos, post-lesson questions, programming assignments, quizzes, office hours, and tutoring.

## Conclusion

Lesson videos and post-lesson questions were the most effective learning tools. These built-in course resources help the most students consistently.

Programming assignments are helpful but slightly less impactful. Office hours and tutoring show lower ratings, possibly because fewer students use them regularly.

Improving core materials like videos and practice questions would likely have the biggest impact on student learning.
